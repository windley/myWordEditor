Welcome to MyWord Editor!

MyWord Editor is a simple way to create beautiful web essays. Example.

You can always backup all your essays, in a portable JSON-based format, so it's easy to switch to another service. The server for MyWord is open source. We hope to encourage other developers to create simple editing environments, so you don't have to be locked into a silo.

MyWord was written by Dave Winer, who also developed the original blogging, RSS feeds and podcasting software.

Please sign on to Twitter to access your stuff. We only use Twitter for identity. We won't read any of your posts, or post anything on your behalf.
